What I'm doing now

I am based in Helsinki, Finland (UTC+02:00).

I am a recent graduate in MSc Service Design and Engineering at Aalto University (4.91/5.00 GPA). This is a double-degree program with a minor in Innovation and Entrepreneurship, which is organized by the EIT Digital Master School. I hold the same degree from the University of Trento, where I attended my first year of studies.

I am currently working as a Research Assistant in the group led by Mario Di Francesco at Aalto University. My research focuses on distributed computing on heterogeneous IoT devices. For this I am using Docker Swarm along with custom software. This was the topic of my master's thesis and also of my presentation at DockerCon Europe 2017.

I am also a Teaching Assistant for the Mobile Cloud Computing course at Aalto University. My main contribution is the development of an automated grading system that uses unit tests to calculate student grades. Students upload their Android projects and receive instant feedback based on the behavior of their app and the expected behavior. This is enabled by running headless Android emulators inside Docker containers and using Appium for user interface testing.
