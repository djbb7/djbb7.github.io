About Me

I am Daniel Bruzual, software developer, entrepreneur and lover of the great outdoors. I was born in Venezuela but I have lived in several countries ever since I was young. I am currently based in Helsinki, Finland.

I am studying in the EIT Digital master's programme in Service Design and Engineering, at Aalto University. I am learning about innovation and entrepreneurship, creating digital services, and designing the architecture of the systems that power them. This is a joint programme with the University of Trento, where I was studying last year.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering (Cum Laude) from Universidad Simón Bolívar in Venezuela.

I have a passion for mobile applications and have made several Android apps, for fun (Voz Del Pueblo), for contests (Together App), and for things I needed which didn't exist (ProRemote).

I taught myself HTML to make websites when I was 11 by getting up at 6:00am on weekends when the family computer was free to use. Later, when I was 16, I learnt how to program web applications in order to make a student survey system for my high school. It allowed them to save hundreds of hours of transcription, and provide useful information to teachers. I also co-founded a start-up (TuTallerMecánico), which I left to come study. I'm always coming up with new ideas and thinking of business opportunities.

I did a 5-month internship at Plista GmbH in Berlin, where I was working on recommendation algorithms for their OnSiteRecommendations plugin. We implemented different algorithms (like collaborative filtering), and used different techniques to combine them.

Later I worked in MobileMediaNetworks in Venezuela, where I developed a CMS that allowed print media editors to add augmented reality to their existing publications, alongside an app that allowed readers to view these contents. I have also made the entire web and mobile applications for TuTallerMecánico, a qype of car repair garages. I have also worked developing Wordpress themes and plugins.

I am a polyglot, speaking Spanish, English, Italian, Portuguese and German fluently. At the moment, I am learning Finnish.

On my spare time you will find me in a mountain, or making a timelipse, or probably both. I like trailrunning and doing physically demmanding activities. I also have a passion for kayak, having recently completed a 5-day trip around the Aeolian Islands in Sicily.
